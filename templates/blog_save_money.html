Why Use Price Trackers?

Online retailers use dynamic pricing - prices change based on demand, time of day, and competitor pricing. Price trackers help you:

  • Monitor prices 24/7 automatically
  • Get instant notifications when prices drop
  • View price history to identify trends
  • Set target prices and wait for the right moment

Top Money-Saving Strategies

Strategy 1: Set Realistic Target Prices

Set your target price 10-20% below the current price. Most products do drop to this range during sales events.

Strategy 2: Track Multiple Stores

Don't just track one store. The same product might be cheaper on a different platform. Compare prices across Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, and more.

Strategy 3: Wait for Sale Events

Major sales like Big Billion Days, Great Indian Festival, and Diwali offer the deepest discounts. Set trackers a month before these events.

Best Categories for Price Tracking

  1. Electronics: Up to 30-40% savings possible
  2. Fashion: 50-70% off during end-of-season sales
  3. Home & Kitchen: 20-30% average savings
  4. Books & Media: Up to 50% off during sales
